Beefaroni

Description

This is a Simple family recipe of one of my favorite pasta meals
as a kid!

Ingredients

  • Elbow pasta
  • Canned green beans
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato soup
  • Salt&Pepper

Cooking Step

  1. Bring water to a boil, and pour elbow pasta into the water. Cook to your desired
    tenderness. Drain pasta and set aside.
  2. Cook ground beef in a pan, salt and pepper seasoning to taste. Break up the meat as
    it cooks down.
  3. Pour the pasta, tomato soup, and drained green beans into the beef pan. Stir until
    mixed.
  4. Enjoy.
