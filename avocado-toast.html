Avocado Toast

Description

This is one of my favorite things to make on a nice weekend for family breaskfast!

Beautiful sourdough toast, with a great avocado, and egg. What could go wrong!

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread loaf
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Everything bagel seasoning

Cooking steps

  1. Slice the bread to your desired thickness, butter both sides, and cook both side of the bread
    on the skillet over medium heat until the bread is browned.
  2. Reduce pan heat to Medium/Low, grease it up, and crack an egg! Flip when ready.
  3. Slice the avocado on the bread, or mash, your choice! Lay the cooked egg on top, then season.
